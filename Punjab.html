Transportation

Punjab has one of the best-developed road networks in the country. All-weather paved roads extend to most villages

, and the state is crossed by a number of national highways. Punjab also is well served by the Northern Railway—part of the national railway system.

There is an international airport in Amritsar, and regular domestic service is available in Chandigarh and Ludhiana.

Several other airports offer cargo service.
